Welcome to Felix Kanitz

Nestled in the vibrant center of Plovdiv near the artistic Kapana district, Felix Kanitz offers a luxurious retreat for discerning travelers. Our six meticulously designed apartments blend modern comfort with elegant sophistication.

Each space is thoughtfully crafted to provide an exceptional stay, combining premium amenities with the charm of Bulgaria's cultural capital. Whether you're visiting for business or pleasure, our aparthotel offers the perfect balance of privacy and service.
